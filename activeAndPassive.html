Passive & Active Voice

Understanding Active and Passive Sentences / Memahami Kalimat Aktif dan Pasif

Definition / Pengertian

Active voice is a sentence in which the subject performs the action.
Passive voice is a sentence where the subject receives the action.

Kalimat aktif adalah kalimat di mana subjek melakukan suatu tindakan.
Kalimat pasif adalah kalimat di mana subjek menerima tindakan tersebut.

Structure / Struktur Kalimat

  • Active: Subject + Verb + Object
  • Passive: Object + to be + Past Participle (V3) + by + Subject
  • Aktif: Subjek + Kata kerja + Objek
  • Pasif: Objek + to be + Kata kerja bentuk ke-3 + by + Subjek

Examples / Contoh Kalimat

  • Active: The chef cooks the meal.
    Aktif: Koki memasak makanan itu.
  • Passive: The meal is cooked by the chef.
    Pasif: Makanan itu dimasak oleh koki.
  • Active: She writes a letter.
    Aktif: Dia menulis sebuah surat.
  • Passive: A letter is written by her.
    Pasif: Sebuah surat ditulis olehnya.
  • Active: They will finish the project.
    Aktif: Mereka akan menyelesaikan proyek itu.
  • Passive: The project will be finished by them.
    Pasif: Proyek itu akan diselesaikan oleh mereka.
